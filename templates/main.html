Give And
Get Feedback

Peerify lets you exchange essays with a partner so both of you can give and get detailed feedback to make your essays the best they can possibly be. With our messaging and annotation tools, editing is simple.

Match With Other Students

With Peerify, you're matched with college students who also have expertise on the topic of your essay. That way, they can understand and engage fully with your essay, giving you useful advice.

PEERify Your Important Essays

Studies show that essays reviewed by at least one peer are 35% more likely to get A's. Don't let your professor be the first one to read your paper! Modeled after the scientific peer-review process, Peerify makes your essay better.
